Tamnanpar

The thai mean of Tamnanpar is "A Legend Of Forest" (绿光森林). 
          It means green forest.
          When the sun shines on the green plants, it looks like a green forest.
          From enter to restaurant, there are all natural views when you walk pass,
          could not resist taking photos. Select favorite-seat sit down,
          neither next to waterfall or patio, enjoy a delicious thai meal,
          and a fresh cold coconut for lunch, immediately fresh up from tired and hot body.
          At night, romantic candle dinner and lovely singer singing english songs for you,
          what a wonderful moment!.
