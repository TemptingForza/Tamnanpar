Tamnapar

The thai mean of Tamnanpar is "A Legend Of Forest" (绿光森林). It means green forest.

When the sun shines on the green plants, it looks like a green forest.

From enter to restaurant, there are all natural views when you walk pass, could not resist taking photos. Select favorite-seat sit down,

neither next to waterfall or patio, enjoy a delicious thai meal, and a fresh cold coconut for lunch, immediately fresh up from tired and hot body.

At night, romantic candle dinner and lovely singer singing english songs for you, what a wonderful moment!.

Restaurant

Forest legend...living food park Created from the rice fields

It is a restaurant in a natural forest in the middle of Rayong city.


Open for more than 20 years

The restaurant was started at the end of the year.

1997 during Tom Yum Kung Starting from liking nature of Mr. Chairat The president of the company

thinks that if you like it you must do it, you must preserve it, and awaken the spirits of people who love nature.

love the environment So it was created from an empty rice field.

Waterpark

Tamnanpar Water Park is a water park located in the resort.

The water park is divided into

3 areas, namely the children's area, the family area, and the adult area.

The children's area is a water park for children.

There are many water games for children to

play. The family area is a water park for families.

There are many water games for families to play.

The adult area is a water park for adults.

The water park is open from 10:00 am to 6:00 pm. The water park is closed on Mondays.
