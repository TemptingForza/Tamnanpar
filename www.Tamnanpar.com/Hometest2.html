From enter to restaurant, there are all natural views when you walk pass, could not resist taking photos. Select favorite-seat sit down,

Restaurant

Forest legend...living food park Created from the rice fields

It is a restaurant in a natural forest in the middle of Rayong city.


Open for more than 20 years
The restaurant was started at the end of the year. 1997 during Tom Yum Kung Starting from liking nature of Mr. Chairat The president of the company thinks that if you like it you must do it, you must preserve it, and awaken the spirits of people who love nature. love the environment So it was created from an empty rice field. Make it a forest Take unused raw materials such as scrap wood and decorate them beautifully, including trees, rocks, cliffs, fountains, and waterfalls, creating a perfectly complete ecosystem.

In the restaurant section
There are chefs who have been together for a long time, more than 20 years, living together as a family. Parents are chefs Since my children were little until today, I have grown up and come to work at the shop and never go anywhere. Everyone has a spirit of love for nature. Take care of the environment Meticulous attention to every dish Focus on healthy menus Most of the ingredients come from surrounding communities, such as fishing communities that deliver seafood. Khao Sampaothong Community Also sends duck eggs, chicken eggs, plants.

Tamnanpar Water Park

Tamnanpar Water Park is a water park located in the resort.

The water park is divided into 3 areas, namely the children's area, the family area, and the adult area.

Children's Area: A dedicated water park section with many water games for children to play.

Family Area: A section designed for families with various water games and activities.

Adult Area: A specialized section catering to adult visitors.


Opening hours:
10:00 am to 6:00 pm

Closed on Mondays
