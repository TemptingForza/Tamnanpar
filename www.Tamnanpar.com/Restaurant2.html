From enter to restaurant, there are all natural views when you walk pass, could not resist taking photos. Select favorite-seat sit down,

neither next to waterfall or patio, enjoy a delicious thai meal, and a fresh cold coconut for lunch, immediately fresh up from tired and hot body.

At night, romantic candle dinner and lovely singer singing english songs for you, what a wonderful moment!.
